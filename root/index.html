Gotta Catch 'em all

Your goal in this game is to earn as many pokeballs as possible while catching a variety of Pokemon along the way.

To play simply click on one of the 3 glass clumps on the forest, Behind one of those grass clumps is a pokemon that can be caught. Behind another grass clump are two free Pokeballs that you can use to try again, and behind the last grass clump is nothing.
